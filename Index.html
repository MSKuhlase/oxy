About Us

Oxy Gas (Pty) Ltd is a proudly female-owned and black-owned South African business dedicated to providing affordable, reliable, and sustainable energy solutions to rural communities. We specialize in the distribution and supply of Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) and other natural gases to support low-cost, effective energy alternatives.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To drive sustainable development by providing rural communities with accessible, safe, and clean energy solutions through LPG.

Our Vision
